What is StuAth.ai?

StuAth.ai is an intelligent scheduling application designed specifically for student athletes who need to balance their academic commitments with their athletic training and competitions. Our AI creates personalized schedules that optimize your time for both academic success and athletic performance.

Key Features

  • Smart weekly schedule generation
  • Academic class integration
  • Practice and game scheduling
  • Sport-specific workout plans
  • Personalized study strategies
  • Success tips for student-athletes
  • Data persistence and export
  • Responsive mobile design

How It Works

  1. Input Your Information: Tell us about your classes, practices, games, and goals
  2. AI Analysis: Our algorithm analyzes your commitments and preferences
  3. Generate Schedule: Get a personalized weekly schedule optimized for your needs
  4. View Results: Access workout plans, study strategies, and success tips
  5. Export & Save: Download your schedule and save your data for future use

Supported Sports

Basketball, Football, Soccer, Tennis, Swimming, Track & Field, Baseball, Volleyball, and more!

Academic Levels

High School, Undergraduate, and Graduate level support with appropriate study strategies for each level.
